We shall be making use of the excellent textbook

  • CLP-3 Multivariable Calculus (Online version) by Joel Feldman, Andrew Rechnitzer & Elyse Yeager, 

This is a free and open-source textbook and has a wealth of worked examples and exercises. In addition to the material on Differentiation and Integration written by the three authors above, I have added two chapters on Laplace Transforms and Fourier Series, the analytical methods parts referred to in the unit title. The book is available online by clicking on the link above. It has a well designed interface and works well on computers and hand-held devices. If you prefer working with a PDF version then you can use the following link,

  • CLP-3 Multivariable Calculus (PDF version).

This is a large text and we shall not be making direct use of all of it. Guidance will be provided for to the important sections for use in our unit. Also available is the collection of just the exercises from the text (along with some extra exercises) and associated hints, answers and solutions,

  • CLP-3 Multivariable Calculus Exercises (PDF version).
