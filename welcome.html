Welcome to the Multi-variable Calculus and Analytical Methods module. In this module we shall be continuing our study of calculus by extending the single-variable theory to functions of two or more variables. We also study the Laplace transform as a means for solving certain types of ordinary differential equation, and give an introduction to Fourier Series, an important tool in the analysis of periodic functions.

We will make use of a textbook CLP-3 Multi-variable Calculus to supplement the material from lectures. See below for details.

The Assessment for this module consists of a collection of coursework problems that are due for submission in the assessment week after Christmas. More details will follow shortly.

Links to reading guidance, tutorial information, lecture captures and other resources shall be posted in the sections below as we go through the module.
